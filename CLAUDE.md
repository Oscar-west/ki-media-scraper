### Project
A web scraper that gathers Norwegian media articles (leserinnlegg, kronikker, debattinnlegg) and classifies them into:

| ID | Label | Description |
|----|-------|-------------|
| A | Business / produktivitet / hype | AI as tool, efficiency, investments, startups, implementation |
| B | Regulering / juss / compliance | EU AI Act, KI-forordningen, privacy, GDPR, legal |
| C | Geopolitikk / makt / sikkerhet | US vs China, Big Tech power, digital sovereignty, defense |
| D | Samfunn / demokrati / eksistensiell refleksjon | Democracy, polarization, disinformation, AGI risk |
| E | Utdanning / forskning | Schools, universities, academic research, AI competence |
| F | Annet | Doesn't fit other categories |

Purpose: Empirical evidence for NRK Ytring op-ed on whether Norwegian AI discourse is dominated by business/hype framing.
